# Block FP&A Style & Formatting Guide — Key Rules

Source: Block FP&A Style & Formatting Guide (live document)

---

## Numbers & Units

- **Dollars:** $M, $B, $K — no space ($17M, $1.05B, $50K)
- **Units (actives, GPV, etc.):** M or B — no space (58.5M actives, $23.3B)
- **Currency code:** space between code and amount (USD 50)
- **Percentages:** no space (75%, +3.0%)
- **Basis points / points:** space before bps or pts (+5 bps, +1.7 pts)

## Rounding

| Value | Rule | Example |
|-------|------|---------|
| Single-digit number or % | One decimal | 9.8%, $4.2M |
| Double-digit number or % | Nearest whole | 15%, $45M |
| Billions | Two decimals | $1.26B |
| Actives | Always one decimal | 58.5M actives |
| bps/pts in text | Round for optics | 6 bps |
| bps/pts in charts/tables | Keep one decimal | 6.1 bps |

## Signs (Variances & Growth Rates)

- **Text:** Always include +/- even with directional words ("increased +1.7 pts", "below AP by -$38M")
- **Text negatives:** hyphen (-$38M, -5% YoY)
- **Charts/tables negatives:** parentheses (($25M), (5.4 bps))
- **bps/pts in charts:** + for positive, () for negative (+5.4 bps, (5.4 bps))
- **Color coding:** green (#34a853) positive, red (#ea4335) negative — NOT applied for +/- 1%, $0.5M, or YoY comparisons

## Time Periods

- **Text:** Q1 2026, March 2026, H1 2026
- **Charts/tables:** Q1'26, Mar'26, H1'26
- Use Q4 / H1, not 4Q / 1H

## Comparisons

- **YoY:** spell out "year over year" or abbreviate YoY; always abbreviate in charts/tables
- **QoQ:** same pattern — do NOT use "quarter on quarter" or "quarter-on-quarter"
- **vs.:** use "vs." (with period) everywhere
- **vs. AP:** Annual Plan — spell out on first use, then AP; do NOT use Plan/plan as shorthand
- **Outlook:** capitalize; spell out fully (not OL abbreviation in formal docs)

## Capitalization

- "gross profit" — lowercase in body text; capitalize only in charts/tables headers
- "opex" / "operating expenses" — lowercase in body text
- "Adjusted EBITDA" / "Adjusted Revenue" — capitalize "Adjusted"
- Standalone headers: all major words (skip articles, short prepositions, short conjunctions)
- In-line headers: **Bold label:** first word only (e.g., **Square US GPV growth:** paragraph)
- "bitcoin" — always lowercase; abbreviate BTC only in tight spaces

## Key Terminology

| Write | Not |
|-------|-----|
| Annual Plan (AP) | Plan / plan |
| Outlook | OL |
| gross profit | Gross Profit (in text) |
| opex | Opex / Operating Expenses (in text) |
| bitcoin | Bitcoin |
| bottom-up | bottoms-up |
| buyside | buy-side |
| in-line | inline |
| true-up / true-down | trueup / true down |
| Cash App Card | Card / Cash Card |
| Rule of 40 | R40 (do not abbreviate) |

## Lists

- **Bulleted:** for items (not sequences)
- **Numbered:** for ordered steps only
- **In-line:** use (1), (2), (3) within a paragraph

## Punctuation

- Oxford/serial comma in series: Square, Cash, and Afterpay
- Em dash (—) for interruptions — add spaces on both sides
- En dash (–) for ranges (5–10, $15–$20)
- Hyphenate compound adjectives before a noun: lower-than-expected churn
- Do NOT hyphenate predicate adjectives: "churn was lower than expected"
